The OWASP Los Angeles chapter typically meets on the 4th Wednesday every month (except Nov) for dinner. We have a great security-related speaker on an appsec related topic. Join us for a great opportunity to network before and following the talk, while enjoying amazing local food and drinks.

## Participation

The Open Worldwide Application Security Project (OWASP) is a not-for-profit, worldwide organization focused on improving the security of application software. Our vision is "No More Insecure Software" and we strive to make application security visible through education, collaboration, and free tools, so that people and organizations can make informed decisions about true application security risks. Everyone is free to participate in OWASP and all of our materials are available under a free and open software license.
